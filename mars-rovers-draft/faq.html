Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Mars and the rovers.

Why is Mars red?

Mars looks red because of iron oxide (rust) in the soil.

How do rovers communicate with Earth?

They use the Deep Space Network to send and receive signals. It can take up to 22 minutes one way.

How do rovers get power?

Older rovers used solar panels. Curiosity and Perseverance use nuclear power (RTG).

How fast do rovers drive?

Very slow. About 0.14 km/h for Curiosity.
